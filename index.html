Also here's a short story about her :
Jinx, originally named Powder, was orphaned during a battle between Piltover and Zaun.
Along with her sister Vi, they were taken in by Vander, a Zaunite defender. Growing up
in the undercity was tough, and Powder was often criticized for her clumsiness by her adopted
brother Mylo, though Vi always defended her.

Powder, Vi, Mylo, and Claggor decided to break into the Piltover workshop of Jayce Talis
to steal Hextech crystals. Powder mishandled the crystals, causing an explosion that damaged
the building and alerted the Piltover Police Enforcers. This led to a chase back to their home in Zaun.
To protect the children, Vander took responsibility and was arrested.
However, Zaunite crime lord Silco attacked the Enforcers and kidnapped Vander.

The children attempted to rescue Vander, but Vi told Powder to stay behind.
Feeling left out, Powder discovered the destructive power of the Hextech crystals
and created a bomb to help. Unfortunately, the explosion killed Vander, Mylo, and Claggor.
Devastated by the outcome, Vi called Powder a "jinx" and abandoned her. Traumatized, Powder found
solace in Silco, who adopted her and she eventually became the chaotic and destructive Jinx. She constantly
heard the voices of Mylo and Claggor in her head and saw hallucinations of their dead bodies.
